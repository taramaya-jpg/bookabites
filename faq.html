Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are your restaurant's hours of operation?

We are open every day from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We are closed on public holidays.

2. Can I make a reservation online?

Yes, you can make a reservation through our online booking system on the 'Reservations' page. Just select the time, date, and number of people.

3. Do you offer takeout or delivery?

Yes, we offer both takeout and delivery services. You can place an order via our website or call us directly.

4. Can I customize my order?

Yes, most of our dishes can be customized based on your preferences. Please inform us of any specific dietary restrictions or preferences when placing your order.

5. Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian and vegan options available on our menu. Please check the menu section for more details.

6. Is your restaurant kid-friendly?

Yes, our restaurant is family-friendly, and we offer a special kids' menu with smaller portion sizes and child-friendly options.

7. Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering services for events. Please contact us through our 'Contact' page or call us directly for more information and pricing.

8. Can I bring my own wine or alcohol?

We have a selection of wines and alcoholic beverages available in our restaurant, but we do not allow outside alcohol to be brought in. If you have a special request, please contact us in advance.

9. Do you have parking available?

Yes, we have parking available for our guests. There are free parking spaces in front and behind the restaurant.

10. How can I contact the restaurant?

You can contact us through our 'Contact' page on the website, or by calling us directly at [Phone Number].
